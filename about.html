About Me

I am currently a third year graduate student at University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I am advised by Eloísa Grifo and I am interested in commutative algebra and homological algebra. I was born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri, and I received my BS in Applied Math and MS in Mathematics from Missouri State University. I enjoy crocheting, reading, and spending time with my dog, Lulu.
